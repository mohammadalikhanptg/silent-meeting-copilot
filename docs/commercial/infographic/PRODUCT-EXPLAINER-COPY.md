# Product Explainer Copy

Reusable descriptions for the Silent Meeting Copilot product. All claims align with the truth brief. "Silent Meeting Copilot" is a working name pending trademark clearance; substitute the final brand when chosen. No prices or launch dates are stated because none are approved.

## 25-word description

A private, real-time meeting assistant that captures both sides of your conversation, coaches you live on what to say and whether you are hitting your objective, then hands you the record.

## 50-word description

A private, real-time meeting assistant. It captures both sides of your conversation, transcribes them as they happen, and coaches you live: suggested lines, objective alignment, drift alerts, open items and talk balance. For interviews it adds a cited evidence pack. Afterwards you get minutes, transcript and action points.

## 100-word description

Most meeting tools help you after the conversation ends. This one helps during it. A desktop helper captures your microphone and the other side as two labelled channels; a cloud engine transcribes and cleans both in near real time, in English or Hindi and Urdu. As you speak, it coaches you: suggested responses, alignment to your objective, drift alerts, open items and talk balance. In interviews it prompts you on claims to verify and produces a downloadable, cited evidence pack. Privacy is built in: audio off by default, consent per session, hard delete, provider opt-out. Records are yours afterwards.

## One-paragraph executive description

Silent Meeting Copilot (working name) is a private, real-time meeting assistant that supports the person during the conversation rather than only summarising it afterwards. It captures both sides of a live conversation as separate channels, transcribes and cleans them in near real time, and delivers live coaching, suggested responses, objective alignment, drift alerts, open items and talk balance, on the operator's own side. A recruitment-focused interviewer mode adds live prompts for claims to verify and a post-interview cited evidence pack designed as a defensible evidence review rather than an automated hiring decision. The product is privacy-first: audio recording is off by default and consent-gated, with retention limits, permanent hard delete and third-party training opt-out. It is in active commercial build; the launch scope is the base Meeting Coach plus an Interviewer add-on.

## Capability bullets

- Live meeting coaching: suggested responses, objective alignment, stay-on-track drift alerts, open items and a talk-balance meter, steady on long sessions.
- Dual-channel capture and transcription of you and the other side, near real time, English plus Hindi and Urdu including code-mix.
- Interviewer assistant: live claims to verify and competency prompts, plus a downloadable cited evidence pack with a disclaimered traffic light.
- Meeting records: Word minutes, full transcript and named action points, with a session library and review.
- Privacy and control: per-session consent, audio off by default, retention with permanent hard delete, and AI-provider opt-out.
- Optional online meeting bot for per-speaker capture (in development, not yet available).

## Outcome bullets

- Say the right thing in the moment, not after the meeting.
- Keep track of every open question and your own objective.
- Run more balanced, more deliberate conversations.
- Make consistent, evidence-based hiring calls you can defend.
- Keep a meeting record without manual note-taking.
- Show customers the privacy and control you operate under.

## Trust statement

Your conversation stays on your side. Audio recording is off by default and only enabled with consent for that session; retention is time-bound with permanent delete on request, and your content is opted out of training third-party models. Access is secured with a magic link plus an app-based one-time code, and every session starts with a compliance acknowledgement. In interview mode the assessment is evidence-based and disclaimered, a review to inform a human decision, not an automated one, and the coaching never fabricates.

## Usage notes

- Do not present the meeting bot, insights, billing or usage metering as available; they are in development or planned.
- Do not present interviewee coaching or a customer-service vertical as available.
- Do not describe the interview output as a genuine-versus-fake verdict; it is a cited evidence review.
- Do not add prices, launch dates, customer counts or a call to action unless approved.
